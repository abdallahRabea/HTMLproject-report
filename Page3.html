History Of OS:

  • The first development of operating systems to manage tape storage was in the late 1950s.
  • In the early 1950s, the General Motors Research Lab developed the first IBM 701 OS.
  • Operating systems began using disks in the mid-1960s
  • The first Unix OS release was developed in the late 1960s.
  • DOS was Microsoft's first operating system. The 86-DOS software was purchased from an enterprise in Seattle in 1981.
  • In 1985, when a GUI was created and paired with MS-DOS, today's popular Windows OS originated.
